Spread with more Cards


Spread with more cards with analysis: In this case the type of spread (quantity and disposition of cards) varies according to the question.

My name is Anna and I am a 34 years old single woman. I would like to know whether something would happen in my future love life. I met someone, a lawyer who I like very much. He is a very nice person and I would like to have a relationship with him but he is very shy.

Question: Is there a chance for a relationship with him or will I have a relationship with another man in the near future?

Answer

Dear Anna,

Thank you for your trust; I hope my prediction meets your expectations and provides answers to your question. The following spread shows very well what the other party's (in this case your lawyer acquaintance) thoughts and feelings about you and what is projected to be between you both in the future.

As you can see, the top three cards show that your lawyer friend is attracted to you and feels good in your company; he likes spending his time with you. The problem is that he does not know enough about as you do not show your inner self. Therefore he believes you don't take him seriously, even though he wants to have a serious relationship and to find his soul mate.

The cards located in the bottom line, which represent emotions, show that he has no self confidence, that`s why he is very shy and doesn`t dare to take any steps. You are a very attractive women and he thinks that he has no chance and believes there is another man around you - who appears as his rival in this spread - and he fears that this person will seduce you; which is also enforced by your behavior.

The current situation is shown by the three cards on the left of the third line. Both of you are thinking about the other, but because of his self confidence problems there is no developing in this case. He is very closed and doubts you will have an future together.

The three cards at the right side, in the third line, tell that everything depends on you. You have to take the first steps and show your emotions and that you are interested in a serious relationship with him. If you do this, he will feel himself more self-confident, his doubts and inhibitions will disappear and he will be able to open up emotionally with you and you can create a happy and stable relationship.

Good luck!

Love,
Abigail
