Spread with 3 Cards


Spread with 3 Cards: Quick spread for a concrete question.

I could not find a job for more than one year now. I have a university degree in Human Resources and speak foreign languages. I have tried everything, but without success.

Question: I would like to know whether I will find a job in the next months or I if have to wait more even longer?

Answer
The following cards show the answer for your question: Journey, Unexpected Joy and Marriage.



The cards clearly say that the changes will be triggered in the next months and you will get out of this bad situation as a winner. You will find a secure and stable job appropriate to your qualification, where you can develop and progress.
